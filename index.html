About Us

STEEL & CRETE Structural Solutions LLP is a specialist structural engineering consultancy based in Kochi, delivering high-end civil and structural engineering solutions aligned with global practices. With hands-on project experience across India, the Middle East,Europe, and South Korea, our engineers have contributed to prestigious developments including nuclear power plants, high-rise buildings, pre-engineered structures, bridges, and machine foundations.

We specialize in structural design, retrofitting, construction support, and vibration engineering, with a strong focus on optimal, innovative design that reduces material usage and construction cost. Sustainability underpins our approach through efficient structural systems and innovative materials. Dr. Dhileep M. Kaviyad Lead Structural Engineer

Dr. Dhileep M. Kaviyad

Well-experienced in implementing innovative and economical designs for structural systems in collaboration with other stakeholders like architects, contractors, geotechnical experts, MEP, and fire consultants. With nearly 25 years of experience in structural design and retrofitting, he has worked on a diverse set of RCC, PSC, Steel buildings, tanks, towers, machine foundations, and industrial & power plant structures. His expertise includes wind tunnel and shake table tests, aseismic design of nuclear power plants, multiscale modelling, vibration studies on bridges due to high-speed express trains, and sustainable structures. Presently, he is focusing more on the design of composite and/or sustainable structures/architectural components made up from a wide spectrum of materials.
